What is the coupon payment calculator?
The coupon payment calculator is a tool that computes the periodic coupon payment on a bond.
How to calculate coupon payment?
To calculate coupon payment you should use the following formula:
coupon payment = face value of bond * ((annual coupon rate / 100) / number of payments perannum)
Why use coupon payment calculator?
The coupon payment calculator is used in order to determines the periodic coupon payment on a bond.
